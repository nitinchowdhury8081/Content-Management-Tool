Amazing India Tour

Date - All data

India is indeed an incredible country with a rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and a variety of experiences to offer. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the serene backwaters of Kerala, there are countless amazing places to visit and things to do in India. Here's a suggested itinerary for an amazing tour in India.

Delete

Edit

New Delhi

Start your journey in the capital city, Delhi. Explore historical sites like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb. Don't miss the bustling markets of Chandni Chowk and the tranquil Lotus Temple

Delete

Edit

Agra

Travel to Agra, home to the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Visit the Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Delete

Edit

Varansi

Experience the spiritual side of India in Varanasi, one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world. Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony on the ghats of the Ganges River and explore the narrow lanes of the old city.

Delete

Edit

Jaipur

Continue to Jaipur, the Pink City, known for its magnificent forts and palaces. Explore the Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal. Don't forget to indulge in the local cuisine and shop for traditional handicrafts.
